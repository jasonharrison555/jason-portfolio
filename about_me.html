About Me

Hello! I’m Jason Harrison, a passionate and driven individual pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Web Design and Development with a Minor in Graphic Design at Brigham Young University of Idaho, where I’ve maintained a GPA of 3.66. Throughout my academic journey, I’ve developed a strong foundation in both the technical and creative aspects of web design and development. I have a broad skill set in both graphic design and web development, with proficiency in tools like Adobe XD, Illustrator, Photoshop, and After Effects, as well as web technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, MySQL, and WordPress. I’m also familiar with content creation, whether it’s for websites, posters, or digital media, and I bring strong technical and soft skills such as time management, critical thinking, problem solving, and team collaboration. You can explore some of my creative projects on my Behance portfolio. Additionally, I’ve built websites like Bountiful Foods and Chamber of Commerce, which showcase my development skills. I’m always eager to learn new technologies and design trends to stay at the cutting edge of both graphic design and web development, and I look forward to collaborating with others on exciting projects.
